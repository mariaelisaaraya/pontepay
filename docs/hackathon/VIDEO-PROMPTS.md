# PontePay — Guiones de Prompt para Seedance 2.0

> Estructura base: `[Sujeto] + [Acción] + en [Entorno] + camera [Movimiento] + style [Estilo] + avoid [Restricciones]`
> 
> Reglas aplicadas: 60–100 palabras, UN solo movimiento de cámara, descripción de físicas explícita, guía negativa al final, lighting al cierre.
> 
> Formato para pegar en Seedance: copiar el bloque `PROMPT` directamente.

---

## 1. Hero — Landing Page Principal

**Uso:** Video de fondo en `pontepay.ar`, loop suave, 16:9  
**Settings:** 16:9 · 1080p · 15s · Standard

```
PROMPT:
Aerial view of Buenos Aires at golden hour, the Rio de la Plata reflecting 
orange light, camera pulls back slowly revealing the grid of city blocks below. 
Translucent digital particles — glowing USDC coins, soft purple nodes — rise 
from the streets and connect in a sparse network above the skyline, then dissolve 
into the sky. No people visible. Atmosphere: hopeful, calm, expansive. 
Style: Apple keynote commercial color grading, anamorphic widescreen, 
warm amber to deep purple gradient sky. Camera slow pull-out aerial, no cuts, 
single continuous shot. Avoid fast motion, avoid corporate generic overlays, 
avoid lens flare overuse. Backlit golden hour, volumetric haze.
```

avoid fast motion. Dramatic side lighting, bokeh background.

## 8. Pitch Opener — Buenos Aires Establishing

**Uso:** Primer plano del video pitch para PULSO, 16:9  
**Settings:** 16:9 · 1080p · 15s · Standard

```
PROMPT:
Dawn over Buenos Aires. Aerial shot from above the Rio de la Plata, 
mist on the water, the city waking. Camera glides forward toward the 
skyline — Palermo, Recoleta visible. Street level begins to emerge: 
a food vendor opens his cart, a woman checks her phone walking fast,

second skin, then fade. One card appears: "Argentina. #2 en LATAM en cripto." 
Then black. Camera begins aerial pull-forward then transitions to low 
street-level tracking shot in one continuous move, no cuts. 
Style: prestige documentary, ARRI ALEXA, 35mm grain, desaturated blue 
dawn to warm morning amber transition. Avoid CGI feel, avoid tourist postcard. 
Cool pre-dawn blue, natural practical street light sources.
```

## Guía de Uso en Seedance 2.0

### Flujo recomendado

1. **Pegar el bloque `PROMPT`** directamente en Seedance — sin modificar
2. **Settings:**
   - Aspect ratio: el indicado en cada prompt
   - Duration: usar el máximo (15s) para escenas complejas, 8–10s para simples
   - Quality: 1080p para pitch y landing page, 720p para social drafts
   - Mode: Standard (no Fast) para los prompts con física compleja
3. **Si el resultado tiene jitter:** agregar `slow motion, 24fps cinematic` al final
4. **Si la cámara se mueve demasiado:** agregar `minimal camera movement, locked off` 
5. **Para referenciar la UI de PontePay:** subir screenshot de la app con `@PontePay_UI` y agregar `@PontePay_UI visible on screen` en el prompt

### Asset references (si subís imágenes)
